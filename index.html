Game Description

One Epic Knight is an endless runner adventure that takes you deep into a dungeon packed with danger and treasure!
Dodge deadly traps, battle fearsome monsters, and collect legendary loot as you dash forward at breakneck speed.
The further you go, the tougher it gets—can you survive the chaos and claim the ultimate prize?
Strap on your armor, grab your sword, and get ready for an epic run!
